# Who Is It? 亲子朗读扩充文稿（可打印版）

## Cover 封面

**英文原文：** Who is it?

**亲子朗读扩充话术（英文）：** Look at this big shadow on the wall. It has four legs and a long tail. It looks like a dog. The dog is playing with a ball. And look at the children. They are watching the dog. They are standing behind a white sheet, and there is a lamp behind them. That's why we can see the shadow. Do you know this dog? Yes, it's Floppy! Floppy is a funny dog. Let's read this book and see more shadows.

**亲子朗读扩充话术（中文）：** 看墙上这个大影子，有四条腿和一条长尾巴，看起来像一只狗。这只狗在玩球。再看小朋友们，他们在看这只狗。他们站在一块白布后面，身后有一盏灯，所以我们能看到影子。你认识这只狗吗？对啦，是Floppy！Floppy是只搞笑的狗狗。我们一起来看看这本书，发现更多影子吧。

**亲子互动提问（简单低幼提问）：** Q: What animal is the shadow? A: It's a dog.

**亲子互动提问（思考拓展提问）：** Q: Why can we see the shadow on the sheet? A: Because the lamp shines light on Floppy. Floppy blocks the light, so the shadow appears on the sheet.

------

## Page 1

**英文原文：** Who is it? Biff and Chip.

**亲子朗读扩充话术（英文）：** Now we are inside the tent. Look at the tent. It has a lamp inside. The lamp is shining. We can see two shadows on the tent. One shadow has spiky hair. That is Chip. The other shadow has longer hair. That is Biff. They are making shadows with their bodies. Biff and Chip are having fun in the tent. Who do you think will come next?

**亲子朗读扩充话术（中文）：** 现在我们到了帐篷里面。看这个帐篷，里面有一盏灯，灯亮亮的。我们在帐篷上能看到两个影子。一个影子头发竖起来，那是Chip。另一个影子头发长一些，那是Biff。他们在用身体做影子。Biff和Chip在帐篷里玩得很开心。你觉得接下来会是谁呢？

**亲子互动提问（简单低幼提问）：** Q: Who are the two shadows? A: Biff and Chip.

**亲子互动提问（思考拓展提问）：** Q: How do you know which shadow is Chip? A: Because Chip has spiky hair, and his shadow shows spiky hair too.

------

## Pages 2–3

**英文原文：** Mum and Kipper.

**亲子朗读扩充话术（英文）：** Now we see two more shadows. This is Mum and this is Kipper. Look at Kipper's shadow. His arms are up in the air. He looks like a monkey! That's so funny. Mum has something in her hand. Maybe it is a pot or a pan. She is playing too. Everyone is making shadows. And look at Dad in the corner. Dad has a blue blanket and a life buoy. What is Dad going to do? I think Dad has a big surprise. Let's keep reading.

**亲子朗读扩充话术（中文）：** 现在我们又看到了两个影子，是妈妈和Kipper。看Kipper的影子，他的胳膊举得高高的，看起来像一只猴子！太搞笑了。妈妈手里拿着一个东西，可能是一个锅或平底锅，她也在玩。每个人都在做影子。再看角落里的爸爸，爸爸拿着一块蓝色毯子和一个救生圈。爸爸要做什么呢？我觉得爸爸有一个大惊喜。我们继续看吧。

**亲子互动提问（简单低幼提问）：** Q: What does Kipper's shadow look like? A: It looks like a monkey.

**亲子互动提问（思考拓展提问）：** Q: What do you think Dad is going to do with the blanket and the life buoy? A: Maybe Dad is going to make a big costume. He wants to surprise everyone with a funny shadow.

------

## Pages 4–5

**英文原文：** Floppy and a spaceman.

**亲子朗读扩充话术（英文）：** Wow! Look at this big shadow. It looks like a spaceman! The spaceman has a round head and a big, strong body. It looks very tall. But look at Floppy. Floppy is barking at the spaceman. "Woof! Woof!" Floppy's ears are standing up. He is very surprised. He doesn't know who this spaceman is. The children are all looking at the spaceman. They are waiting. They want to know who it is. Is it a real spaceman? Or is it someone they know?

**亲子朗读扩充话术（中文）：** 哇！看这个大影子，看起来像一个宇航员！宇航员有一个圆圆的头和大大的身体，看起来好高。但是看Floppy，Floppy正朝着宇航员汪汪叫。Floppy的耳朵竖起来了，他非常惊讶。他不知道宇航员是谁。孩子们都在看着宇航员，他们在等着，他们想知道那是谁。是真的宇航员吗？还是他们认识的人呢？

**亲子互动提问（简单低幼提问）：** Q: What does the big shadow look like? A: It looks like a spaceman.

**亲子互动提问（思考拓展提问）：** Q: Why is Floppy barking at the spaceman? A: Because Floppy doesn't recognise the spaceman. He thinks it is a stranger. He is surprised and a little scared.

------

## Page 6

**英文原文：** No, it's Dad.

**亲子朗读扩充话术（英文）：** It's not a spaceman. It's Dad! Look at Dad. He looks so silly. He has a big bowl on his head. That's his space helmet. He has a life buoy around his neck. And his body is covered with a colourful sheet. What is that in his mouth? Maybe it's a hose from the vacuum cleaner! Everyone is laughing. Kipper is clapping his hands. Wilf is giving a thumbs up. Dad is so clever. He made a spaceman costume with things from home. Everyone is happy. What a funny Dad!

**亲子朗读扩充话术（中文）：** 不是宇航员，是爸爸！看爸爸，他看起来好傻。他头上顶着一个大碗，那是他的太空头盔。脖子上套着救生圈，身上盖着一块彩色床单。他嘴里叼着什么？可能是吸尘器的管子！大家都在笑。Kipper在拍手，Wilf竖起了大拇指。爸爸太聪明了，他用家里的东西做了一套宇航员服装。每个人都很开心。真是个有趣的爸爸！

**亲子互动提问（简单低幼提问）：** Q: Who is the spaceman? A: It's Dad!

**亲子互动提问（思考拓展提问）：** Q: What did Dad use to make his spaceman costume? Name three things. A: Dad used a big bowl for a helmet, a life buoy around his neck, and a colourful sheet on his body. He also had a hose in his mouth.

## 全绘本总结复习（读完后）

**英文总结（宝宝跟读版）：** We saw shadows. Biff and Chip. Mum and Kipper. Floppy and a spaceman. No! It's not a spaceman. It's Dad. Dad is so funny. Everyone laughs.

**中文总结（宝宝跟读版）：** 我们看到了影子。Biff和Chip。妈妈和Kipper。Floppy和一个宇航员。不！不是宇航员，是爸爸。爸爸太搞笑了。大家都笑了。

**读完后的拓展互动：**

**简单低幼提问：** Q: Which shadow did you like best? A: I like the spaceman / the monkey / Dad best.
